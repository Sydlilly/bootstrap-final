Learn more about the Hogwarts Houses

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by J. K. Rowling. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story arc concerns Harry's conflict with Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic and subjugate all wizards and Muggles (non-magical people). The seven books are filled with thrilling adventures, overcoming obstacles, romance, and humor. They have also been turned into movies so fans can enjoy both reading and watching the story unfold.

Gryffindor

The founder of the house is Godric Gryffindor. The house colors are scarlet and gold with the house animal being a lion. The element associated with the house is fire. The ghost of Gryffindor is Nearly-Headless Nick and the common room is in the Gryffindor Tower.

The characteristics of members include:

  • bravery
  • daring
  • nerve
  • chivalry

Some of the well known members include: Harry Potter, Albus Dumbledore, Ronald Weasley, Hermione Granger, Rubeus Hagrid, Neville Longbottom, Sirius Black, Remus Lupin, Ginny Weasley, Fred Weasley, George Weasley, Peter Pettigrew, Lily Evans, James Potter, Oliver Wood.

Hufflepuff

The founder of the house is Helga Hufflepuff. The house colors are yellow and black with the house animal being a badger. The element associated with he house is earth. The ghost of Hufflepuff is Fat Friar and the common room is in the Hufflepuff Basement.

The characteristics of members include:

  • hard work/dedication
  • patience
  • loyalty
  • fair play

Some well known members of the house include: Cedric Diggory, Pomona Sprout, Nymphadora Tonks, Newton Scamander, Hannah Abbott, Zacharias Smith, Ernie Macmillan, Justin Finch-Fletchley, Susan Bones, Leanne, Teddy Lupin, Hepzibah Smith, Bridget Wenlock, Silvanus Kettleburn, Hengist of Woodcroft, Grogan Stump, Cho Chang, Theseus Scamander, Heidi Macavoy, Tamsin Applebee, Artemisia Lufkin, Eddie Redmayne.

Ravenclaw

The founder of the house is Rowena Ravenclaw. The house colors are blue and bronze with the animal being an eagle. The element associated with the house is air. The ghost of Ravenclaw is Grey Lady and the common room Ravenclaw Tower.

The characteristics of members include:

  • intelligence/knowledge
  • wit
  • curiousity
  • creativity

Some well known members of the house include: Filius Flitwick, Luna Lovegood, Cho Chang, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helena Ravenclaw, Garrick Ollivander, Penelope Clearwater, Padma Patil, Gilderoy Lockhart, Myrtle Warren, Terry Boot, Michael Corner, Quirinus Quirrell, Marietta Edgecombe, Sybill Trelawney, Anthony Goldstein, Roger Davies, Marcus Belby, Millicent Bagnold, Ignatia Wildsmith, Xenophilius Lovegood, Lisa Turpin, Sue Li, Eddie Carmichael.

Slytherin

The founder of the house is Salazar Slytherin. The house colors are green and silver with the house animal being a serpent. The element associated with the house is water. The ghost of Slytherin is Bloody Baron and the common room is in the Slytherin Dungeon.

The characteristics of members include:

  • cunning
  • resourcefulness
  • leadership
  • ambition

Some of the well known members of the house include: Draco Malfoy, Severus Snape, Professor Horace Slughorn, Tom Riddle (aka Voldemort), Bellatrix Lestrange, Lucius Malfoy, Vincent Crabbe, Andromeda Tonks, Pansy Parkinson, Narcissa Malfoy, Gregory Goyle, Blaise Zabini, Dolores Umbridge, Phineas Nigellus Black, Millicent Bulstrode, Marcus Flint, Rodolphus Lestrange, Scorpius Malfoy, Sirius Black, Daphne Greengrass, Regulus Black.
